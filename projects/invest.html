Machine Learning-Driven Quantitative Trading Strategy

In the current market environment characterized by inefficiencies, there is a significant opportunity to predict future movements using historical data and public information. I aim to develop a comprehensive grading system composed of two key components:

Trend Similarity Analysis

This component evaluates the similarity between historical and current market trends. The closer the alignment between past and present trends, the higher the score assigned, indicating a greater likelihood of repeating patterns.

Sentiment Analysis Using Large Language Models (LLMs)

By leveraging advanced LLMs, this system will analyze public information, including both positive and negative news. The severity and impact of these news events will be quantified, contributing to the overall score.

Combining these two aspects, the grading system is designed to provide predictive insights into short-term micro-trends, enabling more informed trading decisions in a volatile market environment.
